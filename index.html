Crafting Excellence Since 2009

Redefining Construction Excellence

Gnanaraj Constructions was founded in 2009 by Mr. Gnanaraj, and we began our major project operations in Chennai from 2015. We specialize in designing and executing complete luxury interiors and exteriors that perfectly match your taste and budget.

We are a friendly and open-minded team of architects whose ideas are inspired by people, culture, and creativity. Our designers constantly explore new thoughts, forms, and styles, turning every project into an opportunity to create something beautiful and meaningful.
